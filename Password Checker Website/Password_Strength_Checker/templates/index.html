Strong Passwords Tips and Tricks

Common Mistakes

Replacing letters with symbols isn’t enough. Avoid predictable patterns and think of unique passphrases.

Guilty Habits

Never store passwords in notes or spreadsheets. Use a password manager to stay secure.

What Makes a Strong Password?

A strong password is long, unique, and difficult to guess. Aim for at least 14 characters with mixed types.

Use a Passphrase

Consider using a passphrase. It’s easier to remember and increases security exponentially.
